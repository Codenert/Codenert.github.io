Bootcamp Learning Plan

My main goal is to land a job as a software developer, I know there are different roles such as front end developer, back-end developer and fullstack developer. I would like to work anywhere in a tech company that offers these roles.

After completing the foundation I know for sure that problem-solving skills is my strength. It is good to know that I have it because at the bootcamp I will use it for the techincal challenges. My limitation that I know for sure is communication and working with people. I will use whatever skills and techniques both from the foundation and the one I am going to learn at bootcamp to strengthen this area.

Non-technical challenge

The biggest non-techinical challenge for me at the bootcamp are communication with people, working people and I also have to admit that talking to big crowd of people is also my biggest challenge.

Human skills I would like to develop

I would like to develop and work on my limitations. I would like to see my communication skill and how I work with people improve. Most importantly, how I can train myself to be better around people so that I can fit in easily in any places such as workplace.

My expectation from the bootcamp team is high, the help and support throughout the bootcamp is what I need. I would like them to help me to be good in both problem-solving and human skills. I would like them also to help me with any techincal challenges and also see them as my guides throughout my learning journey at the bootcamp.

I will utilize everything they provide at the bootcamp, the help and everything. If I do get stuck on the problem I will make sure that I will apply whatever I had learned at the foundation such as taking my break, giving myself time away from the computer and etc. To manage workload in the program, I will make that I apply the things that I values which are time and hard work. Foundation Learning Plan

Long term goal

Programming, creating new things and problem-solving using codes are some of the things that I love and enjoy. My long-term goal is wanting to become a web developer, use tech to solve and create applications.

Human skills

The human skills I would like to develop while attending EDA are mainly my time management skills, improve my productivity, and working effectively with people. I understand that in this field, it is not all about writing codes and knowing everything. However, knowing to code is a good start but it is not enough to set my footstep in the tech industry. Working on these human skills will help me to achieve my long term goal.

My strengths and limitations

Based on my research about myself, I found that I am a hard-working individual that loves to help and work with people.

Hard work I think is very important towards my learning journey and career development. The fact that EDA uses flipped learning strategy so seeing hard work as one of my strength, it will help me to complete this course. Also, I am a person that likes to help and work with people and this will also be helpful since in this course we have to work in teams.

However, this course is very short, those strength will be meaningless if I dont put my time and effort.

Commitment to manage my workload

To effectively manage my workload in this program, I will use what I've learned so far from the foundation. I will use the techniques I've learned such as timeboxing and the Pomodoro technique when working on my task. For every task that I will be given, I make sure I understand what are the learning competencies, I will set my time and do my research on those learning competencies for better understanding but more importantly is staying focus.

When things are building up and starting to get overwhelming I will make sure that I stay focus, take a break when necessary. I will try to get back to it, this will make sure that I stay on track.

How and where will I seek help

Whenever I get stuck on a task I will try do it by myself first. This will help me to improve my problem skills as we are all know, a programmer is a person who can solve problem. I will try seek solutions online first, if I still dont get help then I will either seek help from my cohort or from one of the facilitators

Expect from the facilitation team

I see the facilitation team as my guides along this journey who I can rely on. They are there to provide feedback and help me whenever I stuck on task.
