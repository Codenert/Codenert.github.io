Core personal values

How my culture has influenced my values and identity

Two of the many things I value, are time and hard work. My identity as an I-Kiribati person who migrated to New Zealand, has contributed how I value time and hard work. The lifestyle in Kiribati is easy and relaxing. We relied on the ocean and the land crop for food and we do not worry about other expenses like rent. Although we live a very relaxing life, we were taught to work hard especially when it comes to providing for the family. However, time was not an issue as there will always be plenty of time to do the work. It wasn’t until I moved to New Zealand, that I’ve realised that not only do we need to work hard to pay for rent, food and other expenses but also how time plays an important role in life.
